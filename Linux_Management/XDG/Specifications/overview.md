# XDG Specifications
*Because compatibility is great*

[https://specifications.freedesktop.org/basedir-spec/latest/ar01s04.html](https://specifications.freedesktop.org/basedir-spec/latest/ar01s04.html)
## Theory
XDG has set up a bunch of specifications to create compatibility between different distros. These specifications shall not be infringed!!! They are designed with every use case in mind and shall be treated as the word of god. 
## Specifications
* [basedir-spec](Base_Directory_Specification/basedir-spec.md) - Paths for base directories (mainly for desktops)
